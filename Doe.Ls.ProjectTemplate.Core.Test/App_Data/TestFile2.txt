    <div class="section">
        <h4>Department of Education</h4>
        The NSW Department of Education serves the community by leading the provision of world-class education. The department protects young children by regulating preschool and long day care providers. Once children move into school, we provide them with a world-class primary and secondary education. We also work to advance the wellbeing of Aboriginal people.
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h4>Educational Services Division  </h4>
        <p class="Default">Educational Services Division leads and directs the effective provision of educational, corporate and business services that add value to teaching and learning across more than 2200 public schools in NSW. The Division is responsible for coordinating the development and delivery of quality and responsive policies, processes, services, technologies, or business systems that contribute to maximising achievement for every student in a NSW public school.</p>
<p class="Default">The overarching goal of the Educational Services Division is the strategic, future-focussed planning and quality assurance of service delivery to achieve equity of access and outcomes for every student regardless of where he or she lives or attends school.&nbsp;</p>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h4>Primary purpose of the role</h4>
        Assisting with the financial and administrative operations including personnel, salaries, asset management, corporate credit cards, accounts receivable, accounts payable and purchasing for multiple directorates.
    </div>

    <div class="section" id="keyAccountability">
        <h4>Key accountabilities</h4>
        <ul>
<li>Assisting with month-end and financial year-end procedures and reporting</li>
<li>Assisting with the management of Public Schools NSW portfolio directorates&rsquo; finance, administration and customer services.</li>
<li>Assisting with the preparation of management reports.</li>
<li>Ensuring compliance to the Public Finance &amp; Audit Act, Treasurer&rsquo;s Directions and Administration Policies.</li>
<li>Prepare monthly reconciliations</li>
<li>Providing customer service and responding to enquiries on financial, administration and other matters.</li>
</ul>

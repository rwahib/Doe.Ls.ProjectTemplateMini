Position Description
Division Educational Services Division
Directorate School Services Directorate
Business Unit School Services Ultimo
Position Number 174022
Title Primary Curriculum Advisor
Classification SEO2
Brief Role Statement Providing high level advice and support on matters relating to Primary Curriculum.
Statement of Duties
  • Developing policies and guidelines to support the delivery of curriculum, including welfare and management programs to primary students.
  • Developing, implementing and evaluating professional development strategies for Public Schools NSW staff involved in the delivery of Primary curriculum.
  • Maintaining effective communication with internal and external agencies around Primary curriculum where required.
  • Providing high level advice on issues relating to Primary curriculum through the identification of issues, analysis of data and research.
  • Providing high level support for Public Schools NSW Primary Curriculum initiatives and programs.
  • Responding to enquiries from schools, the community, other directorates and external agencies on Primary curriculum.
  • Working as part of a team to provide advice on and contribute to the development and implementation of initiatives in Primary curriculum.
  • Working collaboratively with relevant stakeholders including BOSTES and ACARA to plan support and monitor the development and implementation of strategic projects across the Primary curriculum.
Selection Criteria
  • Teaching qualifications and recent school based experience within the K-12 range
  • Demonstrated commitment to participating in and managing accreditation processes
  • Proven ability to liaise and negotiate with a range of stakeholders and work collaboratively within and across teams
  • High level written and oral communication and interpersonal skills and experience in the preparation of documents including briefings, reports and correspondence
  • Experience in leading curriculum implementation and demonstrated understanding of current trends, resources, quality teaching & assessment practice, relevant legislation and policies
  • Proven ability to meet deadlines, provide sound advice and work independently
  • Demonstrated ability to build the capacity of staff through the development and delivery of innovative programs and services
  • Demonstrated ability to develop, plan and implement strategic initiatives and innovative practices that support education change for improved student learning outcomes
  • Knowledge of and commitment to the Department's Aboriginal education policies
